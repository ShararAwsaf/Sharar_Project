Device Health : determine if the room is good for you or your device

Device Health is a simple app based on Raspberry Pi that allows user to determine a room's suitability for staying. It also comes with a free breath test to scan your breath's humidity too. Just blow at the heat sensor that's all you have to do.
The main inspiration for the project is ensuring the best possible environment for living of people and their devices. Also, efforts are being made to ensure the maximum device possibility in the room that will not hamper room's suitability for living. Stay safe and enjoy!!!
